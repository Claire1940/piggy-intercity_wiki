<Section icon={Shield} title="Making Choices: Impact on Story and Relationships">

The "choice-driven" aspect of Piggy: Intercity means your decisions matter. Dialogue options, actions taken during quests, and even how you interact with the environment can influence your reputation with various factions and characters, ultimately shaping your unique story path.

### How Choices Influence Your Journey

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="NPC Dialogue Choices">
    Every conversation with an NPC presents an opportunity to influence your relationship with them. Choosing empathetic responses might build trust, while aggressive ones could lead to hostility. These interactions often determine which quests become available or how they play out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Quest Outcome Decisions">
    Many quests offer multiple ways to complete them or present moral dilemmas. For example, you might choose to help one faction over another, or decide the fate of a captured character. These choices can have far-reaching consequences, affecting the game world and future narrative branches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reputation and Faction Standing">
    Your choices contribute to your overall reputation with different groups in Evalia. A high reputation can unlock special vendors, unique quests, or even provide safe passage through otherwise hostile territories. Conversely, a low reputation might lead to ambushes or outright refusal of aid.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="World State Alterations">
    In some cases, your critical choices can permanently alter parts of Evalia. This could manifest as changes in NPC behavior, the availability of certain resources, or even the layout of specific areas. The game is designed to react to your decisions, making each playthrough potentially unique.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Demo vs. Full Version Choices">
Remember that choices made in the **demo version** do not carry over to the full game. Use the demo to experiment with mechanics, but understand that your permanent story begins in the full release.
</Callout>
