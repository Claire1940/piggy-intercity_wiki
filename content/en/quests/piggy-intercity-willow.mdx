To complete Willow's primary questline and maximize your progression rewards, you must follow a specific sequence of actions.   <Step num="1" title="Locate the Silver Paw HQ">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accept the Resource Run">
    Speak with Willow to initiate her first quest. She will request survival supplies, including clean water, canned food, and mechanical components. These items are scattered throughout the School and Lumbermill zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Infected Sector">
    Willow will task you with securing a nearby supply depot. Equip a reliable melee weapon or a handgun. Clear out the infected roaming the immediate perimeter before looting the chest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resolve the Faction Choice">
    Return to Willow with the supplies. You will be prompted to make a choice regarding how the resources are distributed. Your selection changes your standing with the Silver Paw and determines the next quest tier.
  </Step>
</Steps>
