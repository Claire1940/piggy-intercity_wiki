<Section icon={Target} title="Survival Loop: Managing Hunger, Thirst, and Health">

Survival in Piggy Intercity is more than just avoiding the infected; it is a constant battle against biological needs. The hunger and thirst systems are persistent, meaning you must always have a supply of consumables in your inventory. When searching for clues about **piggy intercity mayor bailey**, you will often find yourself far from safe zones, making inventory management a top priority.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Scavenging">
    Search residential buildings in the outskirts for canned food and bottled water. These are the most reliable early-game survival items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Crafting Essentials">
    Build a basic campfire at your base or in designated safe spots. Cooking raw ingredients found in the world provides significantly higher nutrition than scavenged snacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Infection Mitigation">
    Keep a supply of bandages and infection suppressants. If an infected scratches you, your infection meter will rise, slowly draining your health until treated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Inventory Upgrades">
    Prioritize finding or crafting a backpack. More slots mean you can carry lore items like Mayor Bailey's journals without sacrificing vital food or ammo.
  </Step>
