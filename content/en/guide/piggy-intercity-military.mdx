<Section icon={Target} title="Military Faction Quests and Dialogue Choices">

The quest system in Piggy Intercity relies heavily on choice-driven RPG mechanics. When interacting with military personnel, players are not simply accepting linear tasks; they are deciding how their character fits into the broader social landscape of Evalia. Working for the military remnant will alienate independent civilian survivor camps, while helping civilians will lock you out of high-tier military supply lines.

The main narrative thread begins with Sergeant Marcus at the Fort gatehouse. Progressing through this questline requires completing sequential tasks across different parts of the map.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Recruiter">
    Travel to the northern gatehouse of the Fort Outpost. Keep your weapons holstered when approaching the gates to prevent the faction guards from identifying you as a hostile survivor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure the Supplies">
    Travel south to the Lumbermill. Search the locked crates inside the main warehouse for stolen military rations and return them to the Fort.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Resolve the Faction Choice">
    Choose whether to deliver the recovered R&D research files directly to Sergeant Marcus or leak them to the civilian refuge camp in the southern valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Faction Rewards">
    Depending on your choice, speak with your chosen faction leader to collect your unique gear blueprints and establish your permanent reputation rank.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Every dialogue choice shifts your standing with the two main groups. Below is a breakdown of how these choices impact your progression and final rewards.

| Choice Selection | Military Reputation Change | Civilian Reputation Change | Unique Reward Unlocked |

<Callout type="info" title="Reputation Lockout">
