### Item Utility Rankings

Beyond weapons, your inventory will be filled with various tools and supplies. Ranking these helps you decide what to drop when your backpack is full.

1. **Quest-Critical Items**: Never drop these. They are the only way to advance the story and gain permanent attribute boosts.
2. **Medical Supplies**: Serums and medkits are rare. If you find a medical stash, prioritize it over food.
3. **Building Materials**: Necessary for base upgrades but heavy. Only carry these if you are heading directly to your plot.
4. **Scrap and Junk**: Useful for crafting but easily found. These should be the first items you discard for better loot.
