<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Repair and Drive Vehicles">

Finding a car is only the first step. In the post-apocalyptic wasteland of Evalia, almost every vehicle you encounter is broken down, missing vital engine components, or completely out of fuel. To make these vehicles operational, you must scavenge the surrounding areas for specific repair tools and mechanical parts.

Understanding the function of each vehicle part prevents you from carrying unnecessary weight in your inventory. Focus on collecting only what is required for your immediate vehicle target.

| Required Item | Primary Function | Common Spawn Locations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Car Battery** | Powers the electrical system and ignition | Garages, Gas Stations, Toolboxes |
| **Engine Parts** | Restores vehicle engine health and performance | Industrial Zones, Lumbermill, Car Hoods |
| **Gasoline Can** | Provides fuel to keep the vehicle running | Gas Stations, Sheds, Highway Wrecks |
| **Scrap Metal** | Repairs physical chassis damage | Construction Sites, Metal Piles |
| **Wrench** | Essential tool required to install parts | Toolboxes, Workbenches, Shelves |

Once you have gathered the necessary supplies, you must perform the repairs systematically. Rushing the process or attempting repairs while under attack will interrupt your progress and waste valuable time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Perimeter">
    Clear out any nearby infected enemies before interacting with the vehicle. The noise of repairs can attract wandering threats from adjacent streets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Your Wrench">
    Ensure the Wrench is equipped in your active hotbar slot. Without a Wrench, you cannot interact with the engine bay to install salvaged components.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install the Battery and Engine Parts">
    Approach the front hood of the car, open the interaction menu, and place the Car Battery and Engine Parts into their respective slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Pour Fuel into the Tank">
    Move to the rear fuel cap of the vehicle and use your Gasoline Can to fill the tank. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge, as driving consumes fuel over time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ignite the Engine">
    Enter the driver's seat and start the vehicle. Keep your headlights off during the night unless absolutely necessary, as light attracts nearby infected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Maintenance Tip">
Always carry a spare Gasoline Can and at least one unit of Engine Parts in your vehicle's trunk storage. Running out of fuel in the middle of a high-threat zone is one of the most common ways players lose their progress.
</Callout>

</Section>
