<Section icon={Target} title="Navigating Story Choices and Consequences">

The heart of the **piggy intercity factions** system lies in the choice-driven narrative. Every quest in Evalia presents a fork in the road. Do you help the struggling survivors at the Fort, or do you prioritize your own base building by hoarding resources? These decisions are permanent in the full version of the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate NPC Dialogue">
    Approach a character in a hub zone. Read the dialogue carefully, as the game uses a consequence-based system rather than simple "Yes/No" prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Evaluate the Moral Weight">
    Consider the faction's goals. Helping one group might alienate another. Check your current reputation before committing to a major story choice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Objective-Based Tasks">
    Quests often involve exploring dangerous routes or retrieving quest-critical items. Success boosts your standing; failure might lower it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Observe World Changes">
    After a major choice, revisit the hub. You may notice new shop items, different NPC dialogue, or even changes in the physical layout of the base.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Story choices also impact your character's growth. The full version includes more level-up attributes than the demo, and many of these are tied to how you resolve conflicts. For example, diplomatic resolutions might boost your "Charisma" or "Intelligence" attributes, while combat-heavy paths favor "Strength" and "Stamina."

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Base Building and Faction Support">

Your base in Piggy Intercity serves as your personal sanctuary and a reflection of your journey through the **piggy intercity factions**.
