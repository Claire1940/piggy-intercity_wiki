<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies & Combat Tips with the Katana">

Using the **piggy intercity katana** effectively requires more than just button mashing. To survive the harsh environments of Evalia, you must integrate advanced combat maneuvers into your playstyle. 

One of the most critical mechanics is the blocking system. By holding the defensive stance just before an infected strikes, you trigger a perfect block. This reduces damage taken to zero and briefly staggers the attacker, opening them up for a devastating counter-slash.

Additionally, spacing is your greatest asset. Because the katana has a reach of 7.5 studs, you can hit most common infected without entering their attack range. Practice backpedaling while swinging to keep enemies at the tip of your blade.
