### Core Combat Tactics

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Headshots">
    Aiming for the head significantly increases your damage output against infected. Practice your aim to conserve ammo and eliminate threats faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage Ammunition Wisely">
    Ammo is a precious resource. Use your most powerful guns for high-threat enemies and weaker weapons (or melee) for smaller, less dangerous infected. Scavenge every container.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Distance">
    Guns excel at ranged combat. Use this to your advantage by creating space between yourself and the infected. Backpedal and shoot to avoid being overwhelmed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Cover">
    The environment offers plenty of cover. Use obstacles to block incoming attacks and reload safely. This is crucial for surviving longer engagements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Switch Weapons Based on Threat">
    Don't be afraid to switch between your primary gun, secondary pistol, or melee weapon. A shotgun is great for a charging infected, while an assault rifle is better for sustained fire.
  </Step>
</Steps>
