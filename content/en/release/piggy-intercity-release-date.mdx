Surviving your first hour in Evalia requires more than just running away from the infected. Piggy: Intercity introduces a survival loop that demands constant resource management. Unlike the standard Piggy escape maps, you must monitor your vitals while navigating the open world.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Basic Supplies">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find a Melee Weapon">
    Ammo is scarce in the early game. Locate a reliable melee weapon like a pipe or bat to handle solo infected without wasting precious resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the First Quest NPC">
    Quests are your primary source of experience and items. Look for NPCs with dialogue markers to begin your story arc in Evalia.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim a Base Plot">
    Once you have gathered enough materials, find a base plot to claim. This provides a safe zone and a place to store excess loot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Survival is not just about physical health; it is also about your reputation. Every choice you make when interacting with other survivors can change how the world reacts to you.

| Survival Stat | Effect of Depletion | Recovery Method | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hunger** | Reduced stamina and health loss | Consuming food items | **High** |
| **Thirst** | Slower movement speed | Drinking water/soda | **High** |
| **Health** | Character death and respawn | Using bandages or medkits | **Critical** |
| **Infection** | Gradual health decay | Using specialized anti-virals | **Medium** |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Survival Tip">
Always carry at least one bottle of water and one food item. The map is large, and finding a shop or resource node while at 5% thirst can be a death sentence.
</Callout>
