<Section icon={Calendar} title="Converting Official Times to Czech Time (CET/CEST)">
To accurately track **Piggy Intercity** events, you'll need to convert the game's announced times to your local Czech time. The Czech Republic observes Central European Time (CET) during standard periods and Central European Summer Time (CEST) during daylight saving periods.

Here's a quick guide to help you convert:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Standard Time (CET)">
    - **UTC to CET**: UTC - 1 hour (e.g., 10:00 UTC is 11:00 CET)
    - **EST to CET**: EST + 6 hours (e.g., 10:00 EST is 16:00 CET)
    - **When**: Typically from late October to late March.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Summer Time (CEST)">
    - **UTC to CEST**: UTC - 2 hours (e.g., 10:00 UTC is 12:00 CEST)
    - **EST to CEST**: EST + 7 hours (e.g., 10:00 EST is 17:00 CEST)
    - **When**: Typically from late March to late October.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
